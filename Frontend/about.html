Our Mission

We believe every dog deserves a loving home. Happy Tails connects shelter dogs with caring families across the country.

Our Story

Started with One Dog, Now Helping Thousands

Happy Tails was founded in 2020 by a group of dog lovers who saw too many shelter animals being overlooked. We built a platform that makes it easy, fast, and joyful to find and adopt a dog.

Today we partner with 500+ shelters across the United States, helping thousands of dogs find forever homes every year.
