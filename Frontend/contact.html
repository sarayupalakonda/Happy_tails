Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does adoption cost?

Adoption fees vary by shelter, typically ₹5,000-₹30,000. This covers vaccinations, microchipping, and spay/neuter costs.

How long does the process take?

After submitting your application, expect a response within 24–48 hours. The full process typically takes 3–7 days.

Are the dogs vaccinated?

Yes! All dogs on our platform are health-checked, vaccinated, microchipped, and spayed/neutered before adoption.

Can I partner my shelter?

Absolutely! We'd love to partner with you. Use the contact form above or email us at partners@happytails.com.
